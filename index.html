Powering the future of insurance

Inshuwa was built with two objectives in mind; Make it easier for insurance companies to launch digital products faster and make it simpler for insurance to be distributed by creating multiple digital delivery channels. We package all the transactional, regulatory and reporting complexities behind easy-to-use APIs, allowing your team to focus on building great customer experiences.
We firmly believe that the future of insurance lies in how easily insurance players can innovate and streamline their products to meet the needs of customers. Central to this are the product designers, innovators and developers who make it possible for these products to come to life and Inshuwa provides the technology backbone for them to achieve this objective via frictionless and secure APIs.
